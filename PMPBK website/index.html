Organizations

Institute for Leadership in Ministry

The Goal of the Institute for Leadership in Ministry is to provide theological education, leadership training, pastoral and spiritual formation for those who demonstrate the ability or the potential serve as leaders in parishes

Youth

The Youth Ministry in Church aims to engage teens in the activities, mission, work of the church and the larger faith community through a variety of experiences and opportunities. The goal is to encourage youth to actively participate in the life of the church

Social Communication

The Organization of Social Communcation is the group of individuals that manage the communcation technology such as powerpoint and facebook live using the facebook account of the church.

Altar Servers

Altar Servers are individuals who assist the priest during mass and other liturgical ceremonies. They have the opportunity to play a special role in the sanctuary and contribute to the worship experience. To be eligible to be an altar server, a person must have receive their first holy communion.

Sacred Music

Church describes her history of Sacred Music as "The Church's most precious treasure." Those with musical talent who contribute to sacred music of the Liturgy exercise a very important role of helping the people of God assemble for holy mass to sing the praises of God.

Events

Feast Day Celebration

The feast day of holycross celebrates every year September 14, It was the time that the holycross was found in a flood and screwed up in the chapel. Every year, every organizations keep themselves ready to celebrate the feast of holycross to show their love of their own patron and for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Chrismas Celebration

A feast central to the Christian Liturgical year, it is preceeded by the season of advent or nativity feast and initiates the season of Christmastide, which historically in the west lasts twelve days and culminates twelfth night.

Lent

Lent is a period of reflection and repentance in the Christian Church leading up to easter. In the western churches, it starts on ash Wednesday and lasts mass 40 days, excluding sundays, as a way to imitate Jesus's fasting in the wilderness before starting his public ministry. In eastern churches, lent starts on the monday of the 7th week before easter and ends on the friday 9 days before easter, and it's called "Great Lent" that includes saturdays and sundays as less strict fasting days.

Charity

According to Thomas Aquinas, charity is one of the seven virtues in Christian theology. He views it as the act of loving and connecting with God, and believes it is the most important virtue. Additionally, Aquinas believe that charity should also extend to loving one's neighbor.
